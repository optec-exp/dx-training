About TALA

The Only Alliance Dedicated to Aerospace Logistics

Founded in 2012 by QUALITAIR&SEA France, TALA — The Aerospace Logistics Alliance — is the only international network of independent freight forwarders exclusively focused on aerospace logistics and air cargo services.

TALA operates on a glocal model: locally-owned and operated affiliates with global coordination, ensuring true on-the-ground expertise at every station. Members share common standards, real-time communication, and collective know-how to handle the most time-critical cargo situations.

  • AOG (Aircraft on Ground) — 24/7 Emergency Desk
  • Critical Air Freight — Time-sensitive shipments
  • On-Board Courier (OBC) — Hand-carry solutions
  • COMAT & Rotables — Company materials
  • Charter Services — Dedicated aircraft
  • DG / Hazmat — Dangerous goods expertise
  • Customs Clearance — End-to-end brokerage
  • Ocean & Road — Multi-modal solutions
