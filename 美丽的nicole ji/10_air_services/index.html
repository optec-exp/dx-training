Decision Guide
Which Service Do You Need?
Answer a few questions to find the right service tier for your shipment
How critical is your delivery timeline?
Aircraft is grounded — every hour costs tens of thousands
An AOG situation — you need the part to reach the aircraft within hours, not days
Urgent but not a minute-by-minute emergency
Production or operations will halt within 24–48 h if shipment doesn't arrive
High-value / sensitive — custody is as important as speed
The shipment cannot go unattended or through standard cargo holds
Recommended: AOG Service
Your situation matches an Aircraft on Ground emergency. Our AOG desk will activate within 2 hours with a dedicated controller, priority flight booking, and ramp delivery coordination.
Recommended: NFO — Next Flight Out
Your shipment needs to move immediately but not necessarily under a dedicated escort. NFO will secure space on the next departing flight and deliver within 12–48 hours.
Recommended: OBC — On Board Courier
Your cargo requires personal escort throughout the journey. A dedicated OPTEC courier will hand-carry it door-to-door with unbroken chain of custody and live GPS tracking.
What type of cargo are you shipping?
Aircraft spare parts / aviation components
Engine parts, landing gear components, avionics, hydraulic systems
Industrial / manufacturing / pharmaceutical goods
Machine parts, electronics, temperature-sensitive goods, industrial components
High-value, sensitive, or classified items
Jewelry, legal documents, biological samples, diplomatic materials, luxury goods
Recommended: AOG Service
Aviation components, especially those grounding an aircraft, are prime candidates for our AOG service with airline-priority freight booking and ramp-side delivery capability.
Recommended: NFO — Next Flight Out
Industrial and pharmaceutical shipments benefit from NFO's rapid dispatch, express customs handling, and automated re-routing to ensure the fastest possible delivery.
Recommended: OBC — On Board Courier
Sensitive, classified, or irreplaceable items require OBC's personal escort. Your cargo stays in the courier's hands from your door to the recipient's hands.
What level of custody does your shipment require?
Standard priority cargo — tracked but can go in cargo hold
Cargo can travel as regular air freight with milestone tracking
Emergency freight — dedicated handlers at each end
Cargo is critical enough to warrant dedicated agents at origin and destination airports
Zero-gap custody — cargo must never leave a person's hands
Cargo must be physically held or supervised by a trusted person at all times during transit
Recommended: NFO — Next Flight Out
NFO provides the speed of the next available flight with active tracking and re-routing — the right balance of urgency and cost when standard cargo custody is sufficient.
Recommended: AOG Service
AOG service deploys dedicated ground handlers at both the origin and destination airports, ensuring your freight is prioritized and tracked at every transfer point.
Recommended: OBC — On Board Courier
If your cargo needs unbroken human custody, OBC is the only choice. Our courier stays with your shipment as hand baggage from origin door to destination recipient.
